Tottenham Hotspur

Number Two in the Barclays Premier League

Tottenham Hotspur have played incredibly well through out this season. Recently, they tied against the ruthless Arsenal Gunners with a score of 2-2.

Tottenham's young team aren't tiring - in fact, they've actually increased the amount of ground they're covering in Premier League games since the turn of the year. Mauricio Pochettino's tireless side have been described as "animals" by Watford boss Quique Sanchez Flores, while Swansea assistant Alan Curtis praised their relentless attacking in the comeback win over his club last month. And any concerns that Spurs would be unable to sustain their high-pressing, high-energy game through the second half of the season appear unfounded.
